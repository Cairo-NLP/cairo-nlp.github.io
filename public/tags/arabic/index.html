Arabic NLP Tools and Datasets May 21, 2025

Abstract Overview of open-source tools and datasets developed for Arabic morphology analysis, Arabic dialects, lexicographic databases, named entity recognition, relation and event extraction, word sense disambiguation, semantic analysis, synonym generation, hate speech detection, LLM instruction and benchmarking datasets, among others Speaker Bio Professor of Artificial Intelligence and the director of SinaLab for Computational Linguistics and Artificial Intelligence at both, Hamad Bin Khalifa University/Qatar and Birzeit University/Palestine.
